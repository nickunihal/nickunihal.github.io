welcome

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you and family to our home stay. Harithagiri coffee homes offers to you , your dream vacation in God's own country.

The Home Stay is beautifully located inside one of the oldest coffee plantation in Wayanad and is surrounded by majestic mountains, tea plantation and forest area.

We grow organic coffee, peppers, cardamon, turmeric, coconut and seasonal vegetables. You can also find lots of fruit bearing trees in our plantation.

Come and experience the serence beauty of Wayanad with us.

Harithagiri coffee homes is offering you the chance to be part of our family by welcoming you to stay at the bungalow situatated inside the coffee plantation which has an excellent view of the hills and forest on all sides. We bring you the world of comfort and hospitality at affordable tariff for your family and kids.
