welcome

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you and family to our home stay. Harithagiri coffee homes offers to you , your dream vacation in God's own country.

The Home Stay is beautifully located 1km away from Meenmutty Waterfalls, inside one of the oldest coffee plantation in Wayanad and is surrounded by majestic mountains, tea plantation and forest area.

We grow organic coffee, peppers, cardamon, turmeric, coconut and seasonal vegetables. You can also find lots of fruit bearing trees in our plantation.

Come and experience the serence beauty of Wayanad with us.

Harithagiri coffee homes is offering you the chance to be part of our family by welcoming you to stay at the bungalow situatated inside the coffee plantation which can accomodate 8 people and has an excellent view of the hills and forest on all sides. We bring you the world of comfort and hospitality at affordable tariff for your family and kids.

Activities

Plantation Visit

From long walks through the lush coffee meadows, where even the air carries the coffee aroma to experiencing the rich taste and smell of of our coffee beans.

Trekking

For those driven by the desire for adventure, there are lots of challenging and small trekking destinations nearby like chembara peak and Pakshipathalam to name a few.

Visit to Waterfalls

The beatiful Meenmutty waterfall and kanthanpara waterfall are located nearby.

Badminton

We have a badminton court located inside our plantation.
